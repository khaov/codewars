(7 kyu) Number of Divisions

Calculate how many times a number can be divided by a given number.

Example

For example the number 6 can be divided by 2 two times:

  
    1. 6 / 2 = 3
    2. 3 / 2 = 1 remainder = 1

100 can be divided by 2 six times:

  
    1. 100 / 2 = 50
    2. 50 / 2 = 25
    3. 25 / 2 = 12 remainder 1
    4. 12 / 2 = 6
    5. 6 / 2 = 3
    6. 3 / 2 = 1 remainder 1
