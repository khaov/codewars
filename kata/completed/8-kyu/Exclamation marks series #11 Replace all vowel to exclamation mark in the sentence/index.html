(8 kyu) Exclamation marks series #11: Replace all vowel to exclamation mark in the sentence

Description:

Replace all vowel to exclamation mark in the sentence. aeiouAEIOU is vowel.

Examples

  
    replace("Hi!") === "H!!"
    replace("!Hi! Hi!") === "!H!! H!!"
    replace("aeiou") === "!!!!!"
    replace("ABCDE") === "!BCD!"
