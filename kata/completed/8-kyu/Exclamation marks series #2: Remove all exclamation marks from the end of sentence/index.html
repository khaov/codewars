(8 kyu) Exclamation marks series #2: Remove all exclamation marks from the end of sentence

Description:

Remove all exclamation marks from the end of sentence.

Examples

  
    "Hi!"     ---> "Hi"
    "Hi!!!"   ---> "Hi"
    "!Hi"     ---> "!Hi"
    "!Hi!"    ---> "!Hi"
    "Hi! Hi!" ---> "Hi! Hi"
    "Hi"      ---> "Hi"
